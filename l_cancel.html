L-Cancel

About Characters L-Cancel Movement Recovery Stages

L-canceling, short for lag canceling, is an advanced technique that allows the player to move faster than they usually do after landing with an aerial attack. This is done by pressing either the L-button, R-button, or Z-button in 7 frames or fewer (7/60 of a second or fewer) before landing with an aerial move. Doing so allows the player to cut the landing animation time (landing lag) in half, allowing the player to make more follow ups with other moves to allow for combos. The aerial attacks that allow for this to be possible are neutral aerial, up aerial, down aerial, back aerial, and forward aerial.
