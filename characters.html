Melee has 26 characters that are playable. Each character have different attributes associated with them to make them unique from each other. Each character also can within certain weight categories as well, such as heavyweight (Bowser), middleweight (Mario), and lightweight (Jigglypuff). In the competitive side of Melee, players usually choose the characters they play based on combination of multiple factors, including but not limited to, the character's placement on the tier list, their playstyles (offensive, defensive, etc.), how fun they are to play,and being an overall fan of the fan character. These characters are then usually categorized under main and secondary characters for the player.

What is a main?

A main is character that the player uses most often in tournament formats. This can be seen as the players "best" or "favorite" character or the character that gives them the best results in tournaments. Players can solo main (play 1 main character), dual main (play 2 main characters), or main as many characters as possible.

What is a secondary?

A secondary is a character that the player uses the second most often in tournament play. These characters are usually for specific character matchups that the player struggles with his or her main(s).

Tier List

The tier list is used to rank the characters based on how they perform if all players have equal skill. This usually takes tournament results, character potential, and fan voting into mind, when it is created.
